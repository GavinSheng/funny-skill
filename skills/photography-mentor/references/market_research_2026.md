# Camera Market Research 2025-2026

## Major Full-Frame Camera Brands

### Tier 1 (Currently Supported)
- **Canon**: EOS R5, EOS R5 Mark II, EOS R6 Mark III, EOS 5D Mark III
- **Sony**: Alpha A7C II, Alpha A7 IV, Alpha A7R V, Alpha A9 III  
- **Nikon**: Z8, Z6 II, Z7 II, Zf

### Additional Important Brands to Consider

#### Fujifilm
- **X-series** (APS-C): X-T5, X-H2, X-H2S - Very popular among enthusiasts
- **GFX-series** (Medium Format): GFX 100 II, GFX 50S II - Professional medium format
- **Full-frame consideration**: While primarily APS-C and medium format, Fujifilm has strong market presence

#### Panasonic
- **S-series full-frame**: S1, S1R, S1H, S5, S5 II, S5 IIX
- **Strong video capabilities**: S1H is cinema-focused, S5 series popular for hybrid shooters
- **L-mount alliance**: Shares lens mount with Leica and Sigma

#### Leica
- **Full-frame**: SL2, SL2-S, Q3, M11 (rangefinder)
- **Premium market**: High-end professional and enthusiast market
- **L-mount**: Part of L-mount alliance with Panasonic and Sigma

#### Sigma
- **fp series**: fp, fp L - Compact full-frame cameras
- **L-mount**: Full participation in L-mount alliance
- **Unique positioning**: Modular design, cinema features

#### OM System (formerly Olympus)
- **Micro Four Thirds**: OM-1 Mark II, OM-5
- **Not full-frame**: But important in the interchangeable lens camera market
- **Specialized strengths**: In-body stabilization, weather sealing, wildlife photography

## Market Trends 2025-2026

### Key Observations
1. **Canon, Sony, Nikon** remain the dominant full-frame players
2. **L-mount alliance** (Leica, Panasonic, Sigma) gaining traction
3. **Fujifilm** dominates APS-C and medium format segments
4. **Video capabilities** increasingly important across all brands
5. **AI-powered features** becoming standard (subject detection, autofocus)

### Recommendation for Skill Expansion
Given the current skill focus on **full-frame cameras**, priority should be:
1. **Panasonic S-series** - Strong full-frame presence, popular for video
2. **Leica SL-series** - Premium full-frame option  
3. **Sigma fp series** - Unique compact full-frame option

Fujifilm and OM System are important but don't fit the current full-frame scope unless expanding beyond full-frame.
