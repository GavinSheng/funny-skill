# Core Photography Theory / 摄影核心理论

## Exposure Triangle Theory / 曝光三角理论

### What is the Exposure Triangle? / 什么是曝光三角？
The exposure triangle is the most fundamental concept in photography, consisting of three interrelated elements:
曝光三角是摄影中最基础的概念，由三个相互关联的要素组成：

- **Aperture (光圈)**: Controls light intake and depth of field
- **Shutter Speed (快门速度)**: Controls exposure time and motion blur  
- **ISO Sensitivity (ISO感光度)**: Controls sensor sensitivity to light

### Aperture / 光圈
- **Notation**: f/1.4, f/2, f/2.8, f/4, f/5.6, f/8, f/11, f/16, f/22
- **表示方法**: f/1.4, f/2, f/2.8, f/4, f/5.6, f/8, f/11, f/16, f/22

- **Smaller f-number = Larger aperture = More light**
- **f值越小 = 光圈越大 = 进光量越多**

- **Larger f-number = Smaller aperture = Less light**  
- **f值越大 = 光圈越小 = 进光量越少**

- **Depth of Field Impact**: Large aperture creates shallow DOF (background blur), small aperture creates deep DOF (front-to-back sharpness)
- **景深影响**: 大光圈产生浅景深（背景虚化），小光圈产生深景深（前后清晰）

### Shutter Speed / 快门速度
- **Notation**: 1/1000s, 1/500s, 1/250s, 1/125s, 1/60s, 1/30s, 1/15s, 1s, 2s
- **表示方法**: 1/1000s, 1/500s, 1/250s, 1/125s, 1/60s, 1/30s, 1/15s, 1s, 2s

- **Faster shutter = Less light = Better motion freezing**
- **快门速度越快 = 进光量越少 = 冻结动作能力越强**

- **Slower shutter = More light = More motion blur**
- **快门速度越慢 = 进光量越多 = 运动模糊效果越明显**

- **Safe Shutter Rule**: Shutter speed ≥ 1/focal length (full-frame equivalent)
- **安全快门**: 快门速度 ≥ 1/焦距（全画幅等效）

### ISO Sensitivity / ISO感光度
- **Notation**: ISO 100, 200, 400, 800, 1600, 3200, 6400+
- **表示方法**: ISO 100, 200, 400, 800, 1600, 3200, 6400+

- **Lower ISO = Better image quality = Less noise**
- **ISO越低 = 画质越好 = 噪点越少**

- **Higher ISO = Worse image quality = More noise**
- **ISO越高 = 画质越差 = 噪点越多**

- **Native ISO**: Most cameras have native ISO of 100 or 200
- **原生ISO**: 大多数相机原生ISO为100或200

## Composition Theory / 构图理论

### Basic Composition Rules / 基本构图法则
- **Rule of Thirds (三分法)**: Divide frame into 3×3 grid, place important elements at intersection points
- **将画面分为九宫格，重要元素放在交点上**

- **Golden Ratio (黄金比例)**: More natural division ratio (~1:1.618)
- **更自然的分割比例（约1:1.618）**

- **Leading Lines (引导线)**: Use lines to guide viewer's eye to subject
- **利用线条引导观众视线到主体**

- **Framing (框架构图)**: Use foreground elements to frame the subject
- **利用前景元素框住主体**

- **Symmetry & Balance (对称与平衡)**: Create visual balance
- **创造视觉平衡感**

## Lighting Theory / 光线理论

### Light Quality / 光线质量
- **Hard Light (硬光)**: Direct light, creates strong shadows and high contrast
- **直射光，产生强烈阴影和高对比度**

- **Soft Light (软光)**: Diffused light, creates soft shadows and low contrast  
- **散射光，产生柔和阴影和低对比度**

- **Directionality (方向性)**: Front light, side light, back light, top light, bottom light effects
- **顺光、侧光、逆光、顶光、底光的不同效果**

### Golden Hour / 黄金时段
- **1 hour after sunrise** and **1 hour before sunset**: Softest, warmest light
- **日出后1小时**和**日落前1小时**: 光线最柔和温暖

- **Blue Hour**: Before sunrise and after sunset: Cool tones, perfect for city nightscapes
- **蓝色时段**: 日出前和日落后: 冷色调，适合城市夜景

## Color Theory / 色彩理论

### Color Basics / 色彩基础
- **Hue (色相)**: Basic color attribute (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple)
- **颜色的基本属性（红、橙、黄、绿、蓝、紫）**

- **Saturation (饱和度)**: Color purity and intensity
- **颜色的纯度和强度**

- **Brightness/Luminance (明度)**: Color brightness
- **颜色的亮度**

### Color Combinations / 色彩搭配
- **Complementary Colors (互补色)**: Opposite colors on color wheel (red-green, blue-orange, yellow-purple)
- **色轮对面的颜色（红-绿、蓝-橙、黄-紫）**

- **Analogous Colors (类似色)**: Adjacent colors on color wheel, harmonious
- **色轮相邻的颜色，和谐统一**

- **Triadic Colors (三角色)**: Three colors equally spaced on color wheel, vibrant
- **色轮等距的三种颜色，充满活力**
