# Photography Mentor - Usage Guide / 摄影导师使用指南

## English / 英文

### Overview
Photography Mentor is a comprehensive AI-powered photography learning and camera operation assistant that supports all major camera brands including Canon, Sony, Nikon, Fujifilm, Panasonic, OM System, Leica, Hasselblad, Pentax, Ricoh, GoPro, DJI, and Insta360.

### Key Features
- **Structured Learning Paths**: Beginner → Intermediate → Expert progression
- **Universal Photography Theory**: Exposure triangle, composition, lighting, color theory
- **Brand-Specific Guidance**: Detailed operation guides for each camera brand
- **Model-Specific Instructions**: Step-by-step guidance for specific camera models
- **Practical Scenarios**: Real-world shooting situations and problem-solving

### How to Use

#### 1. Systematic Learning (Recommended for Beginners)
Ask for structured lessons based on your experience level:
- "Teach me photography basics as a complete beginner"
- "I'm intermediate, help me understand advanced exposure techniques"
- "Create a 7-day photography learning plan for me"

#### 2. Camera-Specific Queries
Get help with your specific camera model:
- "How do I set up back-button focusing on my Canon EOS R5?"
- "What are the best video settings for Sony A7 IV?"
- "How to use focus stacking on Fujifilm X-T5?"

#### 3. Problem Solving
Solve specific photography challenges:
- "Why are my snow photos coming out gray?"
- "How to fix motion blur in low light?"
- "What settings should I use for indoor wedding photography?"

#### 4. Technical Guidance
Get detailed technical information:
- "Explain the difference between aperture priority and shutter priority"
- "How does ISO affect image quality?"
- "What is the rule of thirds in composition?"

### Supported Cameras
**DSLR & Mirrorless**: Canon EOS series, Sony Alpha series, Nikon Z series, Fujifilm X/GFX series, Panasonic S series, OM System OM series, Leica SL/Q series, Hasselblad X/H series, Pentax K series

**Compact & Action**: Ricoh GR series, GoPro Hero series, DJI Osmo Action series, Insta360 X series

### Best Practices
- Start with the beginner learning path if you're new to photography
- Specify your exact camera model for the most accurate guidance
- Ask follow-up questions to dive deeper into specific topics
- Use practical scenarios to apply theoretical knowledge
